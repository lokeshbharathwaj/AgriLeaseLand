Agricultural lease land

Agricultural lease land refers to land that is rented out by a landowner to a farmer or agricultural business for the purpose of farming or raising livestock. This type of leasing arrangement is common in the agricultural industry, as it allows farmers and ranchers to access land that they may not be able to purchase outright.
